Non-Monetary Donations

We accept food, clothes, blankets, toys, Bibles, toiletries, and any essential living products. Your contribution directly supports individuals and families during our monthly Ark of Hope outreach.

Thank you for partnering with us.

Items we gladly accept

Drop-off and collection options can be arranged. Please contact us to coordinate.

  • Non-perishable food items
  • Clothing (new or gently used)
  • Blankets, towels, and bedding
  • Toiletries & hygiene packs
  • Toys & children’s items
  • Bibles & Christian literature
