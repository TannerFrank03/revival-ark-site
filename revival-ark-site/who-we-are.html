Who Are We?

Vision Statement

To become a vessel of revival across communities restoring broken spirits, rebuilding faith, and raising a generation that walks boldly with God. We envision communities renewed in hope, strengthened in Christ, and united in love.

Our Core Values

Faith

Everything we do is rooted in God’s Word and led by the Holy Spirit.

Love

We reflect the unconditional love of Jesus Christ in our conduct, our service, and our relationships.

Compassion

We serve with empathy, kindness, and a genuine desire to uplift the broken, the hurting, and the overlooked.

Integrity

We operate with honesty, transparency, and accountability in all we do.

Community

We believe in unity — building bonds, restoring families, and creating safe spaces for people to encounter God.

Service

We are the hands and feet of Jesus; our work is an expression of our obedience and calling.
